Biography

Ruixiang Zhao (赵瑞祥) is a second-year Ph.D. candidate at AIMC-Lab, School of Information, Renmin University of China, under the guidance of Prof. Xirong Li. He received the B.Eng. degree from Renmin University of China in 2022. His research focuses on multimedia and video-text retrieval. .
